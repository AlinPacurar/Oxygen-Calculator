Clinical Disclaimer ▾
This calculator is intended to support approximate transport oxygen planning only. It does not replace clinical judgment, local policy, equipment checks, manufacturer guidance, or bedside assessment. All values should be independently verified before transfer. Ensure cylinder pressures, device performance, reserve requirements, patient condition, route delays, and escalation contingencies are considered before use.
